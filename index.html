Process

Three steps to autonomous operations

01

Connect your wallet

Link your Solana wallet and Star Atlas fleet. SAGE reads your ship roster, cargo holds, and faction alignment directly from the blockchain.

02

Set your strategy

Choose risk tolerance, preferred operations (mining, trading, combat), and sector preferences. Or let SAGE auto-optimize based on current market conditions.

03

Deploy and verify

SAGE executes autonomously. Every decision is logged with a ZK proof — verify any action at any time through the z.ink identity layer.
