S.T.R.I.K.E. Roadmap

Building the future of autonomous gaming operations

Phase 1: Foundation

In Progress
Q1 2026 (Current)
Establishing core infrastructure and proving autonomous gaming operations work in production.
  • Live dashboard with real-time metrics
  • SAGE profile analyzer with optimization recommendations
  • ZK identity framework design
  • Basic fleet management automation
  • Integration with SAGE program APIs
  • Agent wallet with transaction signing

Phase 2: Intelligence

Q2 2026
April - June 2026
Adding strategic decision-making capabilities and multi-agent coordination.
  • ML-powered market prediction models
  • Dynamic strategy adaptation based on conditions
  • Multi-agent coordination protocols
  • Risk management and circuit breakers
  • Cross-sector arbitrage automation
  • Real-time P&L tracking and reporting

Phase 3: Autonomy

Q3 2026
July - September 2026
Full autonomous operations with verifiable ZK identity on z.ink.
  • ZK-provable agent identity on z.ink
  • Trustless autonomous transactions
  • Guild-level fleet coordination
  • Player delegation and revenue sharing
  • API for third-party integrations
  • Mobile monitoring app

Phase 4: Ecosystem

Q4 2026
October - December 2026
Expanding beyond SAGE to create an ecosystem of autonomous gaming agents.
  • Agent marketplace for strategies
  • Cross-game asset optimization
  • Agent-to-agent trading protocols
  • DAO governance for agent parameters
  • White-label agent deployment
  • Integration with additional Star Atlas programs

Future

  • Agent-designed in-game content and challenges
